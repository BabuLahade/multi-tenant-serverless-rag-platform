Fintech Corp offers personal loans, business loans, and credit cards.

To apply for a personal loan you need:
- Government issued photo ID
- Last 3 months bank statements
- Proof of income or salary slips
- PAN card
- Address proof

Loan amounts range from 50000 to 5000000 rupees.
Loan tenure is 1 to 5 years.

For KYC verification we require Aadhaar card and PAN card.
KYC process takes 2 to 3 business days.

Credit card eligibility requires minimum monthly income of 25000 rupees.
Credit limit is set based on income and credit score.
